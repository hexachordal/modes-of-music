Introduction to Modes

One who is first introduced to Western Classical Music, often first hears about terms such as "notes", "chords" and "scales". They would hear of such things as scales and chords being major or minor. But what if I were to tell you what you know as major and minor scales is but a leaf on the branch of a wider array of scales. In this document, we will focus those branches of major and minor which are referred to as modes. Here are the modes categorized by their nature:

Major modes: Ionian, Lydian, Mixolydian
Minor modes: Aeolian, Dorian, Phrygian, Locrian
